FAQ

Logging in Sleep and Wake Data
  • Q: How do I log data?
    • A: The date and time selectors automatically default to your current day and time, so just click "Set" when you are going to sleep or waking up.
  • Q: What is the happiness selector?
    • A: From a scale of 1 to 7, choose your happiness level at the time of logging your data, with 1 being the lowest.
Tracking Progress
  • Q: What does the graph show?
    • A: The y-axis is the Happiness level and the x-axis is the date.
  • Q: How can I view the different graphs?
    • A: Use the toggle buttons to display the data you want.
Comparing Data
  • Q: How do I compare data?
    • A: Use the selectors to filter the results you want and click "Get data".
  • Q: Why can I only filter for two categories?
    • A: The idea is to use two categories that match your desired choice and the third would be used for you to compare your data to.
Alarm Clock
  • Q: How do I add a new alarm clock?
    • A: Press the plus button next to "Alarm" in the page header.
  • Q: How do I delete an alarm clock?
    • A: Press the trash can next to an alarm and click "Yes" when the warning pop-up appears.
